Privacy & Support

This page provides privacy information and support details for UkuHz, developed by Risonanza Studio.

UkuHz

UkuHz requires microphone access solely for real-time pitch detection while the app is in use.

Audio is never recorded, stored, transmitted, shared, or used for any purpose other than pitch detection.

Information Collection

Our apps are designed to be enjoyed by people of all ages while keeping privacy simple and respectful.

Our apps are designed to minimize data collection.

Unless explicitly stated within a specific app, we do not collect, store, sell, or share personally identifiable information.

  • No account registration required
  • No user tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party data selling
  • No unnecessary personal data collection

Analytics

Some apps may use limited analytics tools provided by Apple or trusted services to improve app stability and performance.

Any analytics data, if used, is anonymized and cannot directly identify users.
